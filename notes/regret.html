Regret

Victoria Wallner

August 2023

Would I go back in time and change the things I regret?

Would any of those people be happy to see me?

If I lost something, would it still hold the same value that I once thought it did?

There aren't many points in my life that I would want to revisit. I know it wouldn't last so why be happy in that moment only to have it taken away again?

Why go through that pain twice? There's no point in it.

Would I be able to read a letter from my future self, or would there be no one left to write it?

And even if I could read it, would I understand what it said?

Right now, I can't seem to see a future where I am happy.

What part of my stupid life should I change to make it meaningful?

There are many words in the dictionary, but I can't find one that defines me.

Where is it?

What is my purpose in being here feeling this pain for a reason I can't even figure out?

What am I still doing here?
