Free of Suffering

Victoria Wallner

January 2026

The world is so horrible and there is so much suffering. I always wonder, how can I still see it so positively? How can I be so grateful for this? It is too hard. Then I have to think about self, the human being that is suffering right here. What about me? What about this human that is suffering? How can I help myself? If I am not okay, then what kind of help am I? If my mind is not open, how do I expect theirs to be?

Always the student. Have I ever worked with anyone that did not enlighten me?

If I want a friend, I have to find an enemy. Enemies show me. Someone who opposes me has shown me where I have not taken care of myself yet.

How do I end it? How do I end my human being suffering? The ego. Is it true? The ego is so aggressive. The ego cannot comprehend losing anything or experiencing suffering. The ego remembers pain and anticipation. It is so obsessed with making things real.

It makes me so curious. Why is that there? Why is the ego so obsessed with reacting and wanting to make reality real? I am not the body, and the ego argues with that so much. It wants to make everything physical. It is not real. So curious. So curious.

But the ego is always asking, who am I, who am I, after something happens. What is the biggest problem? Where is the most hurt? Where is the most fear? It is the mind, not the physical situation. It is all in the head. It is all in the mind.

Ego just takes over constantly. Life will test the death of the ego and the love affair with self.

The ego cares so much about others. What will they think? Who am I now, after this situation happened? And none of it really is real. All in the head.

Be the human that is free of suffering.
