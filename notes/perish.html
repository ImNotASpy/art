Perish

Victoria Wallner

September 2022

I have come to terms that I will perish never seeing the light of champion.

Some people are meant to do great things - near unstoppable in their resolve to leave the legacy of their accomplishments for the world to see.

Others, like me, are mere stairs, meant to serve as platforms for those who would achieve things beyond the capabilities of those like myself.

It's noble to know one's place, I presume.
