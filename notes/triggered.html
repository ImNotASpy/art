Questions to Ask When Triggered

Victoria Wallner

March 2026

1. What am I scared might be true?

Am I jealous because I am scared that when he talks to someone else, it might mean I am not enough for him?

2. What am I embarrassed might be true?

Am I embarrassed that I need attention and do not want to ask for it?

3. What was I expecting from myself and the other person?

Did we actually agree on those expectations or rules?

4. What do I need right now to take care of myself and the relationship?

A snack. A nap. To be vulnerable. A hug.
