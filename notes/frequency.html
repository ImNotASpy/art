Frequency

Victoria Wallner

June 2025

Frequency is a number of actions in time. The quantum is the most simple value system. It is a wave in time.

Mass is a property of energy. Energy is action. Force is interaction.

Staring at your belly button is romance. We all eat and poo. We are animals.

We have the sense of order. Order has values. Values are in order.

Values are matters of consideration, matters of importance, numbers and actions.

We can sit and think and drink and wee. Wee - aren't we all having a good time as our genes set the values.

We have all heard the same romance as we dance. Frequently, frequency is raved and paved to nothing but staring at the belly button. Nuttin'.

The key to the lock is information. Information is patterns of randomness and order disorganised complexity and organised complexity.

Too much information. We reduce values.

E needs m to express itself. Mass is a property of E. We can't do nuttin without m.

We can romance, after we go to the toilet, wash our hands and face, and have breakfast or fast slowly.

It has all been said and done before.

Later, we will be hungry for information. Will we really die? It sure looks like we will.

Cancer affects some people more than others because of their genetics. They have no choice, but we all want to survive.
