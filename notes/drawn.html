Drawn

Victoria Wallner

August 2023

Then you will continually be in pain, because it certainly is.

It is too much to ask a fish to do something as simple as breathe. Never forget the limitations of each being, and how they came to be. It is wrong to ask a fish to breathe because its life is in the water, not the land.

It is too much to ask honesty from those who cannot experience it, for they have not, or cannot.

Ours is not to ask “why are you hurting me," but “why are you drawn to hurt."
