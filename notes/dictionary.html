Words to Delete from the Dictionary

Victoria Wallner

November 2025 - present

words to delete from the dictionary

• passion
• hope
• should/shouldn't
• try
• wish (verb)
• need (loose)
• deserve
• maybe (hard for me)
• someday
• can't (when it's not a literal inability)
• just
• always/never

1. i want to create a language that embodies self-alignment and integration

Instead of:
"I should be better."
"I want to improve."

Instead of:
"I hope it works."
"I will do what I can."

Instead of:
"I'm trying."
"I'm doing this." or "I'm not doing this."

Instead of:
"I shouldn't feel this."
"I feel this."

Instead of:
"I'm passionate about this."
"I'm purposeful about this."

Instead of:
"I wish this would happen."
"I am moving toward this happening."

deserve framework is bullshit.

hurt people hurt people
healed people heal people

saying you do or do not deserve something doesn't change or alter or help any relationship or situation, it destroys it.

the best thing you can do is become loving.. to love on yourself and it will flow outward to others and it'll start to reflect back to you, not because you deserve it.. but because that's how it works.

instead of
"what did i do to deserve this?"
"or how do i get to a place where i deserve x y z?"

"what do i wanna do about this?"
"how do i wanna feel/get to the place where i feel about that?"
