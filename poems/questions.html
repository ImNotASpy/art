Questions

Victoria Wallner

February 2026

So far, just an invitation away.
Do we even have the same eyes?
What does it mean for you to perceive me?
I cannot be held the way I want to be held.
Do I need to be held?
Will I hold myself?
Choosing is hard. Chase or self.
What would it take for me to choose myself?
Why is that not the default? Why is it not automatic?
Is this the way to move?
Do I define that?
Why is there no guidebook?

Every guidebook only seems to resonate with the one who wrote it.
Timestamps. Written. Enclave. Hidden.
Where is the line between them and me?
If solitude is so rewarding, why do I surround myself with people?
Do people matter that much?
Am I harboring ill intent?
Be honest with myself.
I want to be around people.
I see life. Energy. Learning.
Is learning the only thing I receive from others?
Why am I so drawn to learning through their experiences?
No.
We do not have the same eyes.
